VietNamese Food: 5 Traditional Dishes to Look For in VietNam

VietNam is a country located in Southeast Asia .

Vietnamese cuisine is diverse and influenced by the country's geographic regions .

In the north, dishes are characterized by subtle flavors, featuring freshwater ingredients and a preference for simplicity.

In the central region, spicy and bold flavors are prominent, with a focus on seafood.

Meanwhile, in the south, the cuisine is vibrant and sweet, with an abundance of tropical fruits and the use of coconut in many dishes.
